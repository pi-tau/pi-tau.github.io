Hi there. Here is @pi-tau.

By day I am a software developer writing microservices in Go. And by night I like to implement and train deep learning models. I hold a master’s degree in AI and my interests lie mostly in the fields of reinforcement learning and generative modelling. Feel free to checkout some of my public repos ( RL, VAE, PixelCNN ).

As you can probably tell I don’t often socialize with people, and would rather sit in front of the pc, than go out for a beer (or ten). Anyway, this blog is mostly about AI/ML/DL and my endeavors trying to enter the field. I have found that reading a paper on a given topic rarely gives you the whole picture (not even close). So I try to dig deeper into the whys and hows and occasionally make a blog post about it.
